About the Game

In a team effort involving two designers, three artists, and one engineer, the story of Lily unfolds. She's a young girl facing neglect and mistreatment, finding strength in nightmares filled with creatures that symbolize her fears and past struggles. Guided by a voice like her grandfather's, she confronts these monsters, discovering her own resilience. This project combines deep storytelling with Metroidvania-style gameplay, aiming to immerse players in a journey of self-discovery and overcoming challenges.
