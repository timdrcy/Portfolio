About the Game

This game was created as the final project for my second year of university, completed over the course of seven weeks. Our client was a class of children aged 5-7, and our task was to incorporate their imaginative monster drawings into a fully functioning game. Our team consisted of three artists, two engineers, and two designers, working collaboratively to bring this vision to life. The result is an engaging puzzle game where players assist their monster companions in navigating various lands, ultimately facing and defeating a formidable boss. This project not only showcases our technical and artistic skills but also highlights our ability to work as a cohesive team under a tight deadline.
